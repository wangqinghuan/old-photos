No, it’s not. It’s a recreation of an operation, staged for the photographers. The daguerreotype is one of five made by the photographers, Southworth and Hawes, in the so-called “Ether Dome” amphitheater at Boston’s Mass General Hospital. These daguerreotypes show recreations of the first time ether anesthesia was used during a surgical procedure at the hospital, several months earlier. This particular photo lives at the Getty Museum in LA. I’ve seen it in person and it’s very cool.
