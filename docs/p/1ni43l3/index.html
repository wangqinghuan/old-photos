Context if anyone need.. The image shows the USS Liberty after being attacked by Israeli forces during the Six-Day War on June 8, 1967. The USS Liberty was a U.S. Navy intelligence ship patrolling off the Sinai Peninsula when it was attacked by Israeli jets and torpedo boats. The two-hour assault resulted in the deaths of 34 American personnel and left 171 wounded. Israel claimed the attack was a tragic mistake due to poor visibility, but this explanation has been met with skepticism by U.S. officials and survivors. The incident strained relations between the U.S. and Israel, although Israel later paid compensation for the losses.
